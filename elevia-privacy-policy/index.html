CricketDev Elevia Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Elevia

This policy applies to the Elevia Android app offered by CricketDev.

Effective date: March 29, 2026 Contact: cricketdevhq@gmail.com
Short version: Elevia does not require an account, does not include ads, and does not use analytics or tracking SDKs. Saved quotes, categories, and most settings stay on your device.

1. Who we are

Elevia is developed and published by CricketDev. If you have any privacy questions about the app, you can contact us at cricketdevhq@gmail.com.

2. What Elevia does

Elevia is a quote discovery app. It shows a Quote of the Day, fetches additional inspirational quotes, lets you save quotes you like, and optionally schedules a local daily reminder on your device.

3. Information processed by the app

Elevia stores certain app data locally on your device so core features work properly. This local data can include:

  • saved quotes
  • custom categories you create for saved quotes
  • theme, color, sorting, and filter preferences
  • daily notification settings and selected reminder time
  • cached quotes and quote identifiers used to reduce repeats

CricketDev does not require you to create an account and does not receive this local data from the app as part of a user profile or cloud sync service.

4. Internet access and third-party quote delivery

Elevia uses internet access to request quote content from the ZenQuotes API. When your device connects to that service, standard technical request data may be processed by the third-party service or your network provider, such as your IP address and request metadata. This is a normal part of delivering online content.

Elevia currently uses ZenQuotes only to retrieve quote content. CricketDev does not use the app to build advertising audiences or behavioural profiles.

5. Notifications

Elevia offers optional daily reminders. On supported Android versions, the app may request notification permission if you turn this feature on. Reminder scheduling is handled locally on your device and is based on the time you choose in the app.

If you do not want reminders, you can leave notifications off or revoke notification permission in your device settings.

6. Information Elevia does not intentionally collect

Elevia is not designed to collect the following directly:

  • name, email address, or account credentials inside the app
  • contacts, photos, microphone input, or camera data
  • precise location data
  • payment information
  • advertising identifiers for in-app ad targeting

7. Sharing of information

CricketDev does not sell your personal information through Elevia. Because saved quotes and settings are stored locally, they are not routinely shared with CricketDev.

Limited technical information may still be handled by third-party services involved in delivering quote content or platform notifications as part of normal app operation.

8. Data retention and deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete locally stored app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from your device.

9. Security

We aim to keep Elevia lightweight and privacy-conscious, but no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is perfectly secure. Please use the app with that in mind.

10. Changes to this policy

If Elevia's data practices change, CricketDev may update this policy. The updated version will appear at this page with a new effective date.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or support related to Elevia, email cricketdevhq@gmail.com.
